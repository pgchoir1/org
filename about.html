About PGC

Porch of Grace Choir started in 2006 when Rev. Emeka Eze was transferred to Assemblies of God Church (PTI Road Branch).
Formerly, the Choir in the Church was the Adult Choir (Amazing Grace Choir) which normerly sings Hymn, cordinated by the Late Deac. Odior with Mr. Francis Okoroafor as the Assistant Choir Master.
As time went on, the need for the Youths' Choir arose and Rev. Emeka Eze spoke to Bro. Blessed to raise the Choir. So, Bro. Blessed looked for people that were in the previous Youths' Choir and brought them together to start up with about 10 members. It then grew from that to 17, and about 20, 30, 40 and began to grow widely in a space of one year. They began to minister from Church to Church, Programme to Programme (including District Levels). God used Bro. Blessed to move the Choir to that level.
On Saturday 28th July, 2012, he handed over the position of the Music Director to Bro. Obedience Ighama. God also used him mightily to another level. He pioneered the Choir with innovative programmes (especially the first Choir's 3-year Programme; OPEN HEAVENS 2013). This programme brought together exclusive worship with Chris Morgan and some other worshippers.
On Sunday 10th October, 2014, MD Obedience Ighama handed over the position to Bro. Tunde Adesami. In MD Tunde's time as the Music Director of the PGC, he has led the Choir through another dimension of ministry. PGC had tremendious achievements via this Innovative, Gifted, Godly, Giver and Inspirer; which includes the annual Cultural Day, Praise Sunday, Carol Service, End-of-Year Party/Get-Together, OPEN HEAVEN 2016 (with Rev. Igho of the Glorious Fountain Ministries & Other Great Worshippers). Also, it was in his time as the MD that the Choir got an Official logo, facebook page, instagram page, twitter account, blogs and this website. Moreso, Bro. Tunde still have great plans for the Porch of Grace Choir.
PGC is planning to go on air and also release her musical albums as time goes on. It is expedient so anticipate the launch.
