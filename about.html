About PGC

Porch of Grace Choir was founded by the youths choir of the Assemblies of God Church, PTI Road Branch by Bro. Blessed Omole (As the Music Director then). Taking over from the adult choir and hence, the name, Porch of Grace Choir (PGC). The Music Director then relocated as the Head Pastor (Rev. Emeka Eze) of the Church was transferred and Bro. Obedience Ighama became the next Music Director. After innovating and organizing the program (Open Heavens 2013), he relocated and the Choir then came toger again and elected a new MD called Bro. Tunde Adesami. PGC ministered (and is still ministring) in various Churches, and is planning to go on air very soon by the grace of God. PGC also has plans of releasing her musical albums as time goes on. It is expedient so anticipate the launch.
